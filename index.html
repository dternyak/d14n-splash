Joule is a browser extension that brings the speed and ease of Lightning transactions directly to your browser.

By directly connecting your node to web apps, micro payments are made seamless and easy. And by integrating with WebLN, web apps can go beyond simple payments.

TipHub aims to bring sustainable funding to open source projects through tipping with Lightning. Payments are made directly to maintainers Lightning nodes, with no platform fees.
